Chanchal Rajput Poems

Yun Chhod Kar

"Tum gaye yun chhod kar
Jaise boonde badal ko
Tum gaye yun chhod kar
Jaise bhavare kaliyon ko

"Tum gaye yun chhod kar
Jaise jhadte ped se patte ho ...
Tum gaye youn chhod kar
Jaise dhoop thande raaste ko

"Tum gaye yun chhod kar
Jaise maa ne chhoda ho sisakte bachche ko
"Par ab jab tum mile to milna ese
Jaise nadi me leheren ho"

"Tum milna ese
Jaise do jaan ek jism me thehri h
Tum milna ese
Jaise thand me dhoop sunehri ho
Tum milna ese
Jisse na deri ho "

Teri Chhuti Saans

"Teri har chhutti saans
Mujhe me ghul tujhe
Mujh se jod deti h
Tumhare jaane ki aahat sun"

"Meri ruh dum tod deti hai
Tum raho ass paas mere ...
To har roshini zindagi se
Vaasta jod leti h"

I Can't, I Can

"I can't make you feel
But yes I can make you realize
I can't make you smile
But yes I can make you cry"

"I can't stop you
But yes I can let you go ...
I can't make you feel my presence
But yes my absence will hurt you"

"I can't show it
But yes I can reveal it
I can't have it
But yes I can steal it"

"I can wait until this life ends
But I will wait until hope knocks the door
I can see the sunset
But I will wait for the moon"

Tum Vo

"Tum vo pehle to nahi the
Jo mere itne kareeb the
Tum vo pehle to nahi the
Jo mere naseeb the"

"Tum vo pehle to nahi the
Jo itne haseen the ...
Tum vo pehle to nahi the
Jo mujh par mare the"

"Tum vo pehle to nahi the
Jinki Zulfo pr hum fisale the
Tum vo pehle to nahi the
Jinki aankhon mein hum base the"

"Haa Tum vo the jo
Meri sanso mein ghule the
Haa Tum vo the jo
Meri khushbu se mile the
Haa Tum vo the jo
Mujhe uss khuda se dua me mile the"

The Day

"The day you smiled
You almost made me die
The day you replied
I was high as sky"

"The day you came closer
I wanted the exposure ...
The day you lie with me
I get to know you lie to me"

"The day I really wanted to die
But then I smiled
And realized that how high were I
Thinking that you were my sky."

Bachpan

"Bachpan mera sbse achha dost tha
Or uss bachpan me mera ek dost tha
Jitna humnein sath guzara bachpan tha
Usse kahi lambi umar intezar ki thi"

"Bachpan ki dosti se lekar
Ladakpan k yaraana tak sab ...
Tha humare beech or ab
Duriyon se lekar Galatefehmiyon tak
Sab h hmare beech"

"Bachpan ki nok jhok se
Lekar ladakpan ki khatti-meethi
Ladaiyon tak sab tha hmare beech
Or ab ladaiyon se lekar takraar tak
Sab h hmare beech"

"Bachpan ki shaitaniyon se lekar
Ladakpan ki masoomiyat tak
Sab tha hmare beech
Or ab chaal se lakar chalaki tak
Sab h hmare beech"

"Pr ab bhi us dost se lekar
Uski dosti tak sab h mere paas
Ab bhi uski baaton se lekar
Uski yaadon tak sab h mere paas"

"Ab bhi uski khushboo se lekar
Uski aahat tak sab h mere paas
Ab bhi uske door jaane se
Milne tak ka intezaar h mere paas"

"Ab bhi ummeed se lekar koshish
Karne tak ka haq h mere paas
Ab bhi uske jaane se lekar
Aane ka haq h mere tumhare paas"
