Usse Likhna Nahi Aata

Yun to saare lekhak overthinker hote hain, par saare overthinker lekhak nahi hote. Theek aisi hi hai wo hi. Jab tak kalam haath mein or copy paas mein na ho kayi kisse gadh chuki hoti h wo apni duniya mein. Sapne dekh chuki hoti hai ek aisi rachna ki jisne poori duniya ko sochne pe majboor kar diya ho.
... Par jaise hi kalam uthati h, saari rachnayein gayab ho jaati h uske dimaag se. Ab naam rachna ho jaane se koi mahaan rachankar thode hi ho jaata h. Pr uss muyi ko kaun samjhaye, usne to sapne dekh rakhe hein or ussi mein zindagi ki basar bhi shuru kr di hai. "Ari oo rachna, zra ghar k kaamon mein bhi thoda haath bantaya karo. Ye kya haathon mein copy kalam liye firti rehti ho. Likhti to ek shabd bhi nhii. Sasural jaogi to saas ko kya muh dikhaogi? Yahan kaam nhii seekhti, kal ko saas sasur or nanad taane dein to daudi daudi ghar ko na bhaag aana. Jageh nahi milti byaahi beti ko mayeke mein.", maa ne khari khoti ki bauchhar lga di har roz ki tareh. "Haan aati hoon maa", bol rachna apne sapne se nikal maa ka haath bantane pahunchi. Aur yun hi kaam kaaj mein aaj ka din bhi nikal gya, saath hi beh gayi uski hzaron kahaniyan uske kaam k saath saath. Raat jab saare kaam khatm kar wo bistar tak pahunchi, to pairon se uski kalam takrayi, copy bhi thodi door hi giri hui thi. Jaldi jaldi usne unhe uthaya or sar se lgakr kuchh budbudaya. Subeh kaam k chakkar mein inhe to sahi jageh rkhna hi bhul gyii thi. Aadatan usne fir se kalam uthaya or aaj copy k pehle panne pr hi likh diya, " Usse likhna nahi aata." Kuchh yun hi shuru ho gya RACHNA se RACHNAKAR banne ka uska ye safar.

Dus Saal Baad

Mall mein kuchh jyada hi bheed thi aaj. Dhanteras ka mauka tha aur aisa koi bhi nhi tha jise lakshmi apne ghar lane ki jaldi naa ho. Issi bheed mein achanak dono ka hath takraya. Unhone ek dusre ko dekha aur anjane bann apne apne raste pe chal diye.
... Uss ek touch aur nazaron ke mel ne purani sari yaadein taaza kar di thi. Waise toh kuchh khas rishta nhi tha unka. Par jo bhi tha anmol tha unke beech.
Kuch 10 saal pehle ki kahaani hai, jab dono mile the. Baadh ke kaaran aayi vipda mein dono ek NGO ki orr se volunteering kar rhe the. Logo ki madad karte karte dono ek dusre ke kareeb aa chuke the. Iss rescue operation ke dauran ek dusre ki chhoti chhoti zaruraton ko pura krte krte unhe khud pta nhi chla tha ke wo ek dusre ki zarurat bann gye the. Puri team ko yeh baat pta thi. Aur shayad unhe bhi par unhone ise maanne se manaa kar diya. Iss ek-aadh mahine ke sath mein unhe yeh toh pata tha ke dono k beech ek aisa rishta bann chuka h jise koi naam dena uss rishte ki beizzati hogi. Aur unhe ek-dusre ke sapne ka bhi pta tha aur yeh bhi ke dono chahe kitni koshis kr le raste unke kabhi milenge nhi. Unka rasta unn parallel lines ki tarah the jo sirf infinity pe jaakr hi milti h. Khair Rescue Operation khatm hone ki kagaar pe aa chuka tha aur dono ka sath bhi chhutne wala tha. Ek aakhiri baar dono mile aur aise mile ke dono ne ek dusre ko khud mein basa liya tha zindagi bhar ke liye aur fir alag ho gye fir kabhi naa milne ki kasam kha kr.
Ab wo mall k baahar aa chuki thi aur ice-cream ki zidd kr rhi uski beti ki pukaar ne use anayas hi vartamaan mein laakr khada kar diya. Muskurate hue usne bachchi ko ice-cream dilayi. Aur wo bhi nikal pade apni manjil ki orr, iss aas ke sath ke shayad fir kabhi kisi dhanteras pe unka hath yoon hi kisi mall mein takraye.

Sanitizer

Unwillingly I went to the medical to buy some medicines for my mom. Yes, I am an introvert so I like being indoor. And particularly that day was too much hot. On the medical, I saw an old lady who lives in our neighborhood but doesn't really talk to anybody. So even I didn't bother and bought the medicines. She was there the whole time in daze.
... It's like she wanted to say something. Soon after she gave up and called me slowly. I heard and looked around at her questioningly and surprisingly both. She then asked if I had any sanitizer with me as she didn't have enough money to buy at that time. Quickly I gave her the bottle which I keep always with me. From the looks of it, the lady was quite hungry. After applying sanitizer, she ate sandwich from her tiffin. I waited patiently for her to finish. She started telling about herself, how she is all on herself as she lost her husband in army and two sons in an train accident. She was returning from the post office that day after receiving false hope instead of her husband's belongings. We then started walking towards our destination of the day while I let her held my hand for support. The sanitizer had indeed done the ice-breaking between us. While walking she kept talking about her old happy days with her family and friends, how they'd spent their time caring and loving each other. She did even revealed some romantic incidents of her with her husband. The sun was indeed the same, but I wasn't feeling much hot now. And now I even have a friend of whole different world from me. When we reached her door she smoothly stroked my hair and kept my hands in hers and told, 'Young lady, it's nice talking to you. I wish we'll make a good bond. Ohh and by the way you can call me Sarita aunty. That lady doesn't sound good.' I was shocked after listening her last line. She then said that she had overheard me sometimes calling her THAT LADY. And a sweet smile appeared before me. And thus my friendship with the sweetest lady started with the help of a sanitizer ??

Two Finger Test

Wo ek sarkaari hospital cum medical college mein behoshi ki halat m laayi gayi thi. Ek karaah k saath uski behoshi tooti. Usne paaya wo aspataal k ek badbudaar kamre mein gande bichhaune p leti hui hei or uske shareer p ek chaadar k siwa or koi kapda na tha. Wo to apne dost k janmdin ki party mein thi, fir uski aankh lagi or ab wo iss ... gande se kamre mein kyu hai usse kuchh yaad nahi. Poora badan dard se fta jaa rha tha, par seene or neeche k ango mein dard asahniye tha. Or nochne kaatne k nishaan to kayi the uske shareer pr. Usse kuchh samajh nhii aa rha tha k uski ye haalat kisne ki or kyu ki. Issi udhedbun mein fansi uski nazar darwaaze p gayi jahan kuchh 4-5 log baatein kr rhe the. Thodi hi der m wo andar aaye. Ek lady doctor apne 4 vidyarthiyon, jinme 2 ladke bhi the, k saath andar aayi or unhe uski case study karwaane lagi. Unn paanchon ko laga wo ab tak behosh hai. Uss doctor ne bejhijhak uske shareer se chaadar hata diya. Tabhi wo chillayi, "Ye kya kar rhi hein aap? Or mein yahan kyu hu? Hua kya h mere saath?". Sharmsaar hui apne haathon se khud ko chhupane ki ek naakaam koshish ki usne. Par uss doctor ne besharmi or tallakhi k saath jawab diya, "Rape hua h tera. Ek se jyada logon ne kiya hai. Or ab shaant ho jaa or mujhe mera kaam karne de warna iss hospital se bahar finkwa dungi or koi lene bhi nahi aayega iss waqt. Vese naam kya h tera?" "Ji, D..d..Devi.", sakpakate hue usne jawab diya. Uss doctor se fir apna muh fera or unn charon ko batane lagi,"Aaj ham kisi bhi ladki ki virginity ka test karna seekhenge. Isse two finger test kehte hein. Isme do ungaliya ladki k yoni k andar daal kar uske hymen ka pata laga skte hain.", kehte hue usne apni ungaliyan uske andar daal di or unhe samjhaana zaari rkha. Devi k muh se cheekh bhi nahi nikal rahi thi. Poore samay ek laash ki tareh padi rahi. kuchh dhai ghante baad ab kamra vaapas shant ho chuka tha. Uss doctor ne sbko seekha diya tha, saath hi sbko ek ek karke practice bhi krwa di thi. Iss diniya mein devi ka ek bewde baap k siva or koi na tha, padhai mein achhi thi so ek NGO uski padhai or rehen-sehen ka kharcha utha rhi thi. Abhi umar hi kitni hui thi uski, agle maah 15 ki hone waali thi. Par iss lagaatar hui 2 durghatnaon ne uska zindagi se muh tudwa diya tha. Jab doctor hi bhagwaan ka chola pehen haiwaan ka roop dhar le, to fir koi kis par hi bharosa kare. Ye sab sochte hue uski nazar bagal k table pe rakhi blade ki taraf gayi. Or chand minuton mein Devi uss dard or ghrina se door apne Maa k paas jaa chuki thi.
